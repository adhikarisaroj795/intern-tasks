The PMP (Project Management Professional) certification is a globally recognized credential offered by the Project Management Institute (PMI). It validates a professional's ability to manage projects and teams, demonstrating expertise in project management methodologies and best practices.

The benefits of PMP certification include:

  • Career Advancement: Increased job opportunities and higher earning potential.
  • Industry Recognition: Validates expertise in project management.
  • Networking Opportunities: Access to a global community of professionals
